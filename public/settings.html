Our Mission

At Vyapar Sahayak, we believe that small businesses should focus on growth, not get bogged down by complex compliance procedures. Our AI-powered platform simplifies GST filing, tax calculations, and regulatory compliance, making it accessible to entrepreneurs without specialized accounting knowledge.

Our Story

Vyapar Sahayak was founded by three passionate students who recognized the immense challenges small businesses face with tax compliance.

Our mission is to simplify the complex world of taxation through AI-powered tools that make compliance accessible and stress-free.

We're dedicated to helping small companies focus on growth while we handle the complexities of GST filing and regulatory requirements.
