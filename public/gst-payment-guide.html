Complete GST Payment Guide

1

Login to GST Portal

Visit the official GST portal (gst.gov.in) and login with your credentials using your username and password.

Tip: Make sure you have your GSTIN username and password handy. Use the forgot password option if you've forgotten your login details.
2

Navigate to Payments Section

After logging in, go to the 'Services' menu, select 'Payments' and then click on 'Create Challan'.

3

Fill Challan Details

Select the type of tax (CGST, SGST, IGST, Cess) and enter the amount for each tax head. Verify all details before proceeding.

Important: Double-check the amounts to avoid underpayment or overpayment. Underpayment may attract interest and penalties.
4

Choose Payment Method

Select your preferred payment method from the available options. You can pay through:

Net Banking

Pay directly through your bank's net banking portal

Credit/Debit Card

Use your Visa, MasterCard, or RuPay card

UPI

Pay using UPI apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, etc.

NEFT/RTGS

Make payment through NEFT or RTGS from your bank

5

Make Payment

Complete the payment process through your chosen method. You will be redirected to your bank's payment gateway for authentication and completion.

Tip: Keep your debit/credit card, net banking details, or UPI app ready before starting the payment process.
6

Download Payment Receipt

After successful payment, download and save the Challan (CPIN) and payment receipt for your records. This serves as proof of payment.

Important: Always keep a copy of the payment receipt for future reference and during GST audits.
7

File Your GST Return

After making the payment, proceed to file your GST return (GSTR-3B). The payment will be automatically reflected in your return.

Important Points to Remember

  • GST payments are due by the 20th of the following month
  • Late payments attract 18% interest per annum
  • Keep your Challan number (CPIN) safe for future reference
  • Reconcile your books with GST payments regularly
  • Maintain proper documentation of all payments for at least 6 years

Need Help?

If you face any issues during GST payment, you can:

  • Contact GST Helpdesk: 0120-4888999
  • Raise a ticket on the GST portal
  • Contact your bank for payment-related issues
  • Consult a GST practitioner for complex issues
